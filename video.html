Diverse Diet


Date: 2020

Dimension: 1920 x 1080 video

Mediums: 0"1"24, HD color video, Adobe Premiere Pro

Description: For this project, I utilized the kitchen in my home as filming location. I filmed my own with my family's diverse diet at home, with the combination of Chinese hotpot and local steak. In my mind, the food culture plays a very significant role in my life. I want to express the differences between eastern and western food cultures. It also reflects family relationships, real-life, culture conflict, and etc, during the quarantine time.




Exit， Video As Art Installation


Date: 2020

Dimension: 1920 x 1080 video

Mediums: 0"3"04, Adobe Premiere Pro，Video, Music, Ink, Glass

Description: For this video, my purpose is to describe that environmental pollution exists everywhere in the world and our ecological environment is getting worse. I got inspiration from photographer Alberto Seveso, who captured clouds of ink underwater. Therefore, I decided to use ink rendering as a metaphor for air chemical pollution. I injected the acrylic paint into the water with a syringe needle. Like clouds underwater, the acrylic paint slowly covered the village, the woods, giving a sense of suffocation. Hopefully I have a chance project this video in a large rectangle glass if I have a chance in the future.
