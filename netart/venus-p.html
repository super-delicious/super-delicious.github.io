VENUS ENTERS PISCES
February 10 – March 6, 2018

Are you ready for some romance in your life?
VENUS the planet of LOVE rules over our love life and our personal values, including money, is shifting gears from detached Aquarius and into more feeling, compassionate Pisces today.

Our perspective changes in our relationships with other folks as we don’t see things in black and white, but instead live and in color. The Goddess of Love in Pisces means we tend to express our love in a more embracing way, so we are better at forgiving and understanding others, and we can show more compassion to our partners and close friends. We also are more vulnerable and more easily seduced, but can be seductive ourselves. Pisces is a very sexual sign and energy for all of us.

Our hearts are more open as we see the beauty in others, which makes this a perfect time to meet someone new or to strengthen the spiritual bonds of an existing relationship. It’s a good time to spend time with those you love. Love doesn’t need to be expressed with words as much as we can intuitively just know what others are feeling!

With Venus in poetic Pisces, we tend to feel more tender and affectionate, but we still may be hard to reach on some levels, because there’s a stronger desire for something that we have a hard time understanding ourselves or defining for others, and therefore we are more difficult to satisfy.

Borders and boundaries slowly dissolve under the Venus in Pisces transit, and unfortunately, this allows the more shady characters to take advantage. Some folks may be victimized or deluded in some way, so we must watch for those who are more evasive, persuasive, and manipulative.

Pay attention to this influence, everything isn’t what it seems in love and around our finances.

~Ruby
