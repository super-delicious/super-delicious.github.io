Mini Game: Whack-A-Mole


Coding, 2020

It's a stress relief game, "whack-a-mole." My game was inspired by the Japanese whack-a-mole arcade game, in which you need to move the mouse to hit it. Once the highest score is reached, the game is over. And as the level increases, the color of the monochromatic background intensifies.
