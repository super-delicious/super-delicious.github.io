Artist Statement



Yixin Cao is a digital media artist who explore how the technology changed the art world. She combined human being traditional art and technology to explore multimedia such as Maya, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, Touch Designer, and Coding.

Yixin raised in Zhejiang, China and moved to Riverside, CA, US. In 2015. She is currently a student at San Jose State University, majoring in studio practice. She has attended studio art programs with a passion for painting, drawing, sculpting. Her curiosity makes her on track to keep learning at an early age for art. After she took Art 74 and Art 75 courses in Spring 2020 at SJSU, she has found academic interest, which is digital media art. She is currently pursuing a BFA in Digital Media Art at San Jose State University.
