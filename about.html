Artist Statement


Yixin Cao is a graduating student majoring in art at San Jose State University. During her college year, she utilized various techniques and tools to express ideas and emotions artistically. She explored various mediums such as Maya, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, Touch Designer, and Coding, etc.
Through experimental video, virtual and augmented reality art installation, she explored gender discrimination, identity, culture conflict, and eco-awareness. To overcome these issues, she spent lots of effort creating narratives and atmospheres of the digital space and building a virtual realm to examine our reality. She believes digital media artworks bring the audience sense of interaction and empathy, though it is a virtual reality environment.
She is also a good listener and willing to cooperate with co-workers, take the essence and make a comprehensive summary and decision. She is currently pursuing a BFA in Digital Media Art at San Jose State University. She looks forward to the opportunity to learn and grow as a Digital Media Art student, a future artist mainly engaged in the digital art field and with industry-standard tools and techniques, and a member of the community.
