Description

Ocean man was surfing on Malibu beach when he suddenly crashes and loses his surfboard and is now walking on the beach to find it. The game controls Ocean man as he jumps and moves toward, running across the sand and searching for his surfboard, finding the board and achieving his goal, and eventually, the Jump man can surf in the ocean.

Game Objects

Ocean man, crabs, surfboard

Sounds

Beach music, surf sound, crab pinch sound

Controls

Jumping moving forward, backward, upward, downward

Game flow

The game flow is a typical 2d platformer, heavily inspired by a Mario game's flow. Therefore, the player chooses the pace at which the game is played.

Levels

It is a normal level and possibly a boss fight level.
