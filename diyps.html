DIY Photoshop


Coding, 2020

INSTRUTCTIONS


Click and press mouse on canvas to draw.

Hit 's' to save image

Hit 'e' to erase image

Hit "1": black brush; "2": grey brush; "3": circle face; "4": eye; "5": smilling smouth; "6": pupil highlight; "7": light circle face; "8": unhappy mouth; "9": flower; "0": square; "b": green line

Description


I was inspired by the artist Takashi Murakami, his work “Flower with Smiley Face.” Each of these emoji seems to have its own feelings and personalities. These characters are expressed in super flat aesthetics. I strongly suggest people utilize and enjoy these tools to draw emoji expressions representing their personalities and feelings.
