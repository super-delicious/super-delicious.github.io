Social Distance Shelter


Maya 3D Modeling & Rendering, 1920x1080, 2020

My inspiration originates from the fact that the epidemic is a contagious disease that requires people to keep a social distance. This motivates me to create a virtual space as the scenery of spiritual healing for security purposes. It is also the revelation of the reality of environmental degradation. I proposed and completed a prosthetic architecture to stay inside a comfortable and safe environment, hoping to reduce the chances of people being exposed to the outside, which is harmful. I used face extrusion, bevels, soft selection, sculpting tools, & patch extrusions tools in Maya. The three frames of the sphere are extended by one surface of a cuboid. The ball's three semicircles of lights I use detach tool to slices it. I used the extrude tool to assemble the sphere's interior from one surface at the bottom up. My lighting tends to create sensory of blazing hot environment and a purplish-grey sky, aiming for constructing an atmosphere of recent California's fire.
