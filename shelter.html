Social Distance Shelter


Maya 3D Modeling & Rendering, 1920x1080, 2020

My inspiration comes from the fact that the epidemic is contagious and requires people to keep a social distance. This prompted me to make a virtual space as the scenery of spiritual healing because I wanted to explore security for spiritual healing. It is also the revelation of environmental degradation and reality. I make a prosthetic architecture to exit a comfortable and safe environment; otherwise, people will be harmful or deadly exposed to the outside. I used face extrusion, bevels, soft selection, sculpting tools, & patch extrusions tools in Maya. The three frames of the sphere are extended by one surface of a cuboid. The ball's three semicircles of lights I use detach tool to slices it. I used the extrude tool to assemble the sphere's interior from one surface at the bottom up. My lighting tends to create sensory of blazing hot environment and a purplish-grey sky to create an atmosphere for California's fire where I live.
