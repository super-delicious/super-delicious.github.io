Dr. XXX


HTML GAME, 2020

Description


I expect things to get better and give players a chance to overcome COVID challenges and life difficulties in the game, even when it's not easy to do in real life. In reality, the shortage of doctors' equipment makes it difficult for them to work and save lives better. An anonymous doctor Dr.XXX who works in a hospital trying to gain more protective suits, masks this protective equipment; the player should dodge the evil waste mask where stain in there. The evil masks also remind people to discard used masks properly. When he obtained a certain amount of equipment, the game down to the next level so Dr.XXX can breathe outside does his hobby, surfing. The goal is to gather more surfboards on the beach to enjoy the second level.
